# Getting Support from Anthropic

If you have worked through the troubleshooting guides and still need help, you can submit a support ticket directly to Anthropic.

---

## How to reach Anthropic support

There are two ways to open a support ticket — use whichever is easiest for you.

### Via your Claude account

1. Log in to your Claude account
2. Click your **initials or name** in the lower-left corner of the app
3. Select **Get help**
4. A support messenger will open — click **Send us a message** to create a ticket

### Via the Claude Help Center

1. Go to [Claude Support](https://support.claude.com)
2. Click the **message icon** in the bottom-right corner of the page
3. The support messenger will open

### In both cases

Once the messenger is open, click **Technical Issue** to connect with a specialized technical support bot.

---

## Before you contact support

To help Anthropic resolve your issue as quickly as possible, gather your system information and logs before opening a ticket.
